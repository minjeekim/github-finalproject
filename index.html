Banana bread recipe

Like banana bread? Try this delicious recipe


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 bananas, finely crushed
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla


Directions (20-30min):

  1. Cream together butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs and crushed bananas.
  3. Combine well.
  4. Sift together flour, soda and salt.
  5. Add to creamed mixture.
  6. Add vanilla.
  7. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan.
  8. Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes.
